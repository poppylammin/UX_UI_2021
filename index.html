#LetUsDance

Website Redesign.

Poppy Lammin

About the cause

A social movement created to ease the lockdown restrictions within South Australia to allow the creative, music and performing arts industry to restart due to the impacts of COVID-19.

Research Key Findings

Unclear website goals

Website is basic and ambiguous, does not fully capture the goal of the cause.

Stressful colour scheme

Overbearing colour theme that is not visually appealing

Human tendancy to sign petitions

Most people,even those indirectly affected, will sign petitions.

Lack of interaction

Those interviewed want an easier flow and more interactive experience

Problem Statement

COVID-19 restrictions imposed on South Australia are taking away the livelihood of those involved in the arts industry. We will raise awareness and get signatures for a petition through a redesign of the current Let Us Dance website.

Iteration Phases

Initial Sketches

As a team, we conducted a 15 minute sketch/brainstorming session to get our intial ideas on to paper.

Wireframes

After further paper iterations, we created low fidelity wireframes. Focussing on the chunking of content and layout of information.

Mid-Fidelity

Using the style guide created for this project, colour, copy, and images were added to the wireframe. Slowly getting closer to our final prototype, and giving enough instruction for our developer.
